Notification Banner

1: Has icon, no content, has customized aria-label "Information"

It will read "Information"

2: No icon, has content

It will read as "2" only. Even having aria-label, it will be ignored.

3: Has icon, has content, has customized aria-label "Number of items"

It will read as "4". Aria-label is ignored.

Case 5: Has icon, no content, no customized aria-label

It will NOT read. The warning log is issued.
